🍼 Privacy Policy / 隐私政策

App: 宝宝-跟踪记录 (BabyLove)

Last updated / 最后更新: April 2026

Data Collection / 数据收集

宝宝-跟踪记录 does not collect any personal data. All information you enter — feeding records, sleep records, diaper records, and growth data — is stored locally on your device only and never transmitted to any server.

宝宝-跟踪记录不收集任何个人数据。您输入的所有信息（喂奶、睡眠、尿布、成长数据）仅存储在您的设备本地，从不上传到任何服务器。

Third-Party Services / 第三方服务

宝宝-跟踪记录 does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

宝宝-跟踪记录不使用任何第三方分析、广告或追踪服务。

Children's Privacy / 儿童隐私

宝宝-跟踪记录 is designed for parents and caregivers to record infant data. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

本 App 专为父母和看护人记录婴幼儿数据而设计，不会故意收集 13 岁以下儿童的个人信息。

Data Security / 数据安全

All data remains on your device and is protected by your device's built-in security features (Face ID, passcode, etc.).

所有数据保留在您的设备上，受设备内置安全功能（面容 ID、密码等）保护。
