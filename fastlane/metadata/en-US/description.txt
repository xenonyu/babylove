Baby Tracker is the simplest, most beautiful app for tracking your newborn's daily routines.

📊 TRACK EVERYTHING
• Feeding — Log time, duration, and amount with one tap.
• Sleep — Track naps and night sleep. Discover the golden sleep window.
• Diapers — Quick log to monitor baby's health.
• Growth — Weight, height, head circumference with WHO growth charts.

🔮 SMART PREDICTIONS
Powered by your baby's own data, the app predicts the next feeding time and sleep window so you can plan ahead with confidence.

📅 MONTHLY & YEARLY SUMMARY
Auto-generated monthly and yearly summaries capture every milestone and growth moment.

📸 MEMORIES
Capture photos and milestones to cherish every first smile, first step, and precious moment.

🌍 Available in Chinese, English, Japanese & Korean.

🔒 All data is stored locally on your device. No account required. Your family's privacy is fully protected.
